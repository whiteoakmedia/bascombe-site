Lawn Care

Our Expertise

Grow Grass Greener

A healthy, vibrant lawn is the foundation of any beautiful outdoor space. Our comprehensive lawn care services are designed to keep your yard looking its best throughout every season. From precision mowing and targeted fertilization to advanced weed control and aeration, we combine proven techniques with expert knowledge to deliver results you can see and feel. Whether you need routine maintenance or a complete lawn renovation, the Bascombe team has you covered.

Our Services

What We Offer

Lawn Mowing

Precision mowing tailored to your lawn's needs, ensuring an even cut and a polished look every time.

Fertilization

Custom fertilization programs that nourish your soil and promote thick, healthy grass growth throughout the year.

Weed Control

Targeted treatments that eliminate unwanted weeds while keeping your lawn safe, green, and thriving.

Aeration

Core aeration to relieve soil compaction, improve water absorption, and strengthen your lawn's root system.

Effective pest control solutions that protect your lawn from grubs, insects, and other damaging organisms.

Lawn Renovation

Complete lawn restoration including overseeding, grading, and soil amendment to bring tired yards back to life.
